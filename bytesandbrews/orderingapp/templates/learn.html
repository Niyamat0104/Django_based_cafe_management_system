Essential Brewing Tips

01

Water Temperature

195-205°F (90-96°C) for optimal extraction

02

Grind Size

Fine for espresso, medium for drip, coarse for French press

03

Coffee-to-Water Ratio

1:16 for pour-over, 1:2 for espresso

04

Freshness Matters

Use beans within 30 days of roast date
